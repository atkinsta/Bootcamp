MF DOOM

About DOOM

Daniel Dumile, more often recognized by his stage name MF DOOM, is a hip hop artist known for his unique style and easily recognizable persona as a "mad villian". DOOM was born in London, England and shortly moved to Long Island, New York. His first music debut was with a group named KMD. In this group, he went by the name Zev Love X. It was not until 1997 that Daniel donned his notorious mask, based on the cartoon super-villian Doctor Doom. It was around this time that MF DOOM released his debut album under this alias titled Operation: Doomsday.

Aliases and Groups

Daniel Dumile raps under many different aliases, with MF DOOM being his biggest and most regonizable. Below is a list of aliases Dumile has used across various albums:

  • MF DOOM
  • Viktor Vaughn
  • King Geedorah
  • Metal Face

In addition to the aliases above, MF DOOM has also formed numerous groups in collaborations with other artists. These groups include:

  • Danger Doom
  • Madvilliany
With each alias Daniel Dumile uses has a different personality which he uses to control the tone, mood, and feel of the albums.
